# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and harassment-free project.
Contributors should communicate constructively, critique ideas and evidence
rather than people, disclose material assumptions, and accept correction when
results or implementations are wrong.

Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
unwanted sexual attention, deliberate intimidation, publishing another
person's private information, or sustained disruption of project work.

## Scope and Enforcement

This standard applies in repository discussions, reviews, issues, and other
spaces where someone represents the project. Report conduct concerns privately
through the repository owner's GitHub profile or, when a security concern is
also involved, through the process in `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when conduct is inappropriate. Reports will be handled
as confidentially as practical, with attention to both safety and procedural
fairness.
